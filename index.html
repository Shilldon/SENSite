Find out about schools in your local area

Here you can find information on the number of schools within postcodes areas, what special needs those schools cater for, ofsted ratings, pupil numbers per school, and mix of boys and girls so you can find the right place for your child.

Simply enter the first part of the postcode you want to search to display details about all schools in that postcode area.

Use the * wildcard at the end of the postcode to search against a range of codes e.g:

RG1 returns all schools in the RG1 post code area.

RG1* returns all schools in postcode areas starting with RG1 which would include RG1 through RG19.

There are, of course, a large number of schools in England. You can limit the results displayed up to a maximum of 50 per page.
